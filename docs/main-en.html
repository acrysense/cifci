Frequently Asked Questions

Cargo transportation needed. How long does it take to calculate the shipping cost?

Cargo transportation. This service is calculated individually. Please, provide information on the cargo parameters to approximately calculate the shipping cost:

- cargo weight and volume. The volume to be calculated by the formula including a coefficient;
- distance. In case of long distance, we consider force majeure circumstances;
- Customs clearance and documents. To be prepared by a specialist;
- Special conditions for transportation of certain goods. For example, perishable goods or liquid dangerous goods;
- The cost of the transported cargo or identical goods to be calculated on a case-by-case basis.

Insurance services. Cargo transportation is not possible without an insurance certificate.

We need to transport large consignment. How does the shipping work, can I track the cargo in real time?

Cargo transportation in ÇiFÇi Trade Logistic company is well-established and coordinated. Our specialists monitor the movement of each cargo regardless a type of transport. We work under contract with the largest ports and stations. We are empowered to contact any station of the final destination and discuss the conditions and time for cargo acceptance, unloading and delivery directly to the recipient. If necessary, our company undertakes to execute all documents including customs.

I need to transport perishable food. How do you transport fruit?

Fruit must be transported only under special conditions. First, it is necessary to secure and control the temperature. To do this, we have special refrigerated vehicles in our fleet. If fruit is to be transported by rail, refrigerated containers are used. The client shall provide information on the fruit harvest date, the term of storage provided for by sanitary standards and transportation conditions.

What is “special cargo”?

Special cargo includes any cargo that requires special transportation conditions. It narrows options of transportation means and increases expenditures on the part of the carrier. Some of such cargoes require compulsory insurance as they enhance the carrier's liability.

Special transportation conditions include:

- temperature control (transportation of perishable goods, medicines, coffins, deceased, etc.);
- the inability to use standard means of transportation (oversized and heavy cargo, self-propelled machines and tracked vehicles);
- increased responsibility or risk of the carrier (transportation of animals, high-value cargo, transplants, flammable and chemical substances, etc.).

Many types of special cargo require increased attention to safety rules. It requires additional expenditures on the part of the carrier and increases tariffs. In certain cases, there shall be provided additional documents to allow carriage of special cargo (for example, when transporting animals, a pet passport with vaccinations recordings and a veterinary permission are required).

What information shall be provided to use your cargo transportation service?

For cargo transportation of any type, the following info shall be provided:

- a full description with the name and characteristics (if there are any features);
- country of origin;
- the delivery point of the cargo and info on customs duties if any;
- dimensions of the cargo;
- volume of goods.

When the info provided you can get competent advice from our specialist, select the best option for transporting goods. Then, the paperwork for the application and transportation will follow – this to be done by our specialist quickly and competently.

What dimensions are allowed for transportation?

It depends on the type of transport. There are certain rules for domestic and international carriage. More accurate information you can obtain from our consultants who answer customer calls around the clock. Only upon clearing up some details, it will be possible to answer about acceptable size load and dimensions. There are exceptions allowing transportation of any oversized cargo.
