WATER/SEA freight transpor

Transportation by sea is good for non-perishable oversized cargo which is rather expensive to transport by other means.

We transport by sea large consignments over long distances without loss of quality.

Advantages of water transport:

We comply with the technologies of loading, placing, fastening loads to secure cargo from rolling, storm vibration, humidity & temperature changes to ensure safety of products being delivered

ÇiFÇi Trade Logistic professionals will analyze your cargo’s characteristics, select the optimal logistics scheme, prepare necessary documents, package and label, insure and load the goods in accordance with regulations and requirements of international cargo transportation as provided for the UN Convention, International (Brussels) Convention for the Unification of Certain Rules relating to Bill of Lading, the Hague-Visby Rules, other legal acts.

International marine transportation

Reusable packaging allows transportation of almost all types of goods.

Advantages of marine transportation:

Wide possibilities to transport large consignments of goods; no restrictions on dimensions or hazard classes; safety, efficiency and reliability; opportunities to make your business flexible in solving logistics problems and reducing logistics costs. Maritime transportation is susceptible to the weather conditions.

Water freight services:

We organize budget-friendly multimodal sea freight transportation to international destinations.
You can consult with our experts free of charge.

Our services:

  • cargo handling
  • stevedoring works;
  • surveying works;
  • collection, storage in warehouses;
  • consolidation of goods at temporary storage warehouses (temporary storage warehouses);
  • CMR notes, customs documentation; insurance;
  • cargo tracking;

We have partner relationships with the largest ports and shipping organizations. It allows us to plan international shipping in the most efficient way, delivering cargo of any type within the shortest possible time. The cost of transportation is calculated individually depending on a type, volume of goods and distance.
