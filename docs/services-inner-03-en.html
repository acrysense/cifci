Transportation of goods by road and truck

Transportation by land is one of the most common types of cargo carriage.

Trade Logistic company transports cargo by land across Europe and Asia and elaborates customized logistical itineraries on a case-by-case basis. Such approach allows us to ensure safety and efficiency of the services provided.

Advantages of transportation by road and truck:

  • Flexible route planning;
  • Door-to-door delivery;
  • Agreement on individual transportation of goods;
  • Fast delivery within the stipulated period;
  • Real-time cargo tracking;
  • Delivery exactly to the specified address.
